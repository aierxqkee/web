Team Tutberidze is the name of the group of figure skaters coach Eteri Tutberidze in the "Khrustal'nyy" department of the Sports School "Sambo-70."


The team of coaches, which includes Eteri Tutberidze, Daniil Gleikhengauz, Sergey Dudakov, has been winning all the medals in women's single skating for many years now. According to the honored Russian coach T.A. Tarasova, they found the key to success and a system of approach to training.

Eteri Georgievna claims that as soon as an athlete descends from the rewarding podium, he becomes nobody and he will have to work again and proves that he deserves attention
